Our History

The World Travel Vacation Agency is founded in 2020 in Prishtinë. World Travel has grown from a small startup to one of the world’s leading digital travel companies. Part of World Traveling Inc. (Vacation Agency). World Travel’s mission is to make it easier for everyone to experience the world.

By investing in the technology that helps take the friction out of travel, World Travel seamlessly connects millions of travellers with memorable experiences, a range of transport options and incredible places to stay - from homes to hotels and much more. As one of the world’s largest travel marketplaces for both established brands and entrepreneurs of all sizes, World Travel enables properties all over the world to reach a global audience and grow their businesses.

World Travel is available and offers many vacations and other unique places to stay with a great prices. No matter where you want to go or what you want to do, World Travel makes it easy and backs it all up with 24/7 customer support.
